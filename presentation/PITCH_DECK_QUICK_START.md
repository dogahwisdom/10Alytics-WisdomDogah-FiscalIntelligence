## üó£Ô∏è Delivery Tips

### Voice
- **Slow down** for big numbers: "$69 billion", "9 times", "2 hours"
- **Speed up** through technical details
- **Pause** after important points
- **Sound natural** - like explaining to a friend

### Body Language
- **Make eye contact** - don't just read slides
- **Use gestures** - point to visuals, use hands
- **Move naturally** - don't hide behind podium
- **Show confidence** - you've done the work

### Handling Questions
- **Listen completely** before answering
- **Repeat the question** so everyone hears
- **Be honest** - if you don't know, say so
- **Connect to your story** - every answer reinforces your message

## üö® Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Don't use technical jargon** - Say "finds patterns" not "K-Means clustering"
2. **Don't read the slides** - You should know the content, slides are just support
3. **Don't rush** - Pause after big numbers and important points
4. **Don't apologize** - You've done great work, own it
5. **Don't over-explain** - Keep it simple, keep it moving
