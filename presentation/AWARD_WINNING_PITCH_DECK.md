## PRESENTATION TIPS FOR DELIVERY

### Voice and Tone
- **Speak like you're talking to a friend**, not reading a script
- **Pause after big numbers**—let them sink in
- **Use your hands**—gesture naturally
- **Make eye contact**—connect with your audience
- **Vary your pace**—slow down for important points

### Body Language
- **Stand confidently**—you've done the work
- **Move naturally**—don't hide behind the podium
- **Smile**—you're sharing something exciting
- **Show enthusiasm**—if you're not excited, why should they be?

### Handling Questions
- **Listen completely** before answering
- **Repeat the question** so everyone hears it
- **Be honest**—if you don't know, say so and offer to find out
- **Connect back to your story**—every answer should reinforce your message

### Time Management
- **Total time**: 10-12 minutes for presentation
- **2-3 minutes** for Q&A
- **Practice with a timer**—know which slides you can skip if needed
- **Have a "deep dive" version** ready if they want more detail
